About

The person behind the byline

"I write to make things clear, and to make them matter."

Hi, I’m Gunjan — a communications professional and journalist with a dual master’s in Journalism from Mumbai University and the University of Galway.

My career spans two continents: digital journalism for 500,000+ readers in India, editorial work at an award-winning outlet in Ireland, and voluntary communications roles for cricket across Connacht.

I also run Ink & Byline — an independent editorial publication covering film, food, tech, and culture. 72 subscribers, 50% average open rate, and a genuine labour of love.

Currently in Galway, Ireland. Open to content marketing, PR, social media, and editorial roles.
